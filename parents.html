Zeus' Parents

Zeus' father was a Titan named Cronus. Cronus was the leader and youngest of the first generation of Titans. He overthrew his father Uranus and ruled until he was overthrown by Zeus.

Zeus' mother was a Titan named Rhea. She was known as the mother of the gods. In order to protect Zeus from being swallowed by Cronus, she birthed Zeus in a cave on the island Crete and gave Cronus a stone wrapped in a cloth instead.
